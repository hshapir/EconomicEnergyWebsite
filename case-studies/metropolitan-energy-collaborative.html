Metropolitan Energy Collaborative

With the end of ComEd’s transition period and favorable frozen rate structures, municipalities around Chicago were being confronted with increases in electricity costs on the order of 35% to 65% as of January 2007.

Economic Energy, as the program facilitator of the Metropolitan Energy Collaborative (a joint effort by the Metropolitan Mayors Caucus of Chicago and enterprise Cook County), has been able to save Chicago-area municipalities an average of 26.9% over ComEd’s tariffs effective January 2, 2007.
