Our Mission

Our Goal: Helping You Save Money

Since Economic Energy's founding in 1997, our mission has been to reduce energy expenses for our clients. What you pay for energy is a function of how much you use, when you use it, how efficiently you use it and how much it costs per unit. We attack energy expenses on all fronts. 

While our mission is unchanging, our focus on which energy savings element to attack at any particular time does vary with market and regulatory conditions. Now, with electricity costs rising as much as 45% in ComEd's service on January 2, 2007, most of our client activity has been securing cost effective electricity supply. 

Every year since October 1999, when retail electricity open access came to Illinois, Economic Energy has helped clients choose the most cost-effective supply for over 250 mW of electricity. Now, Economic Energy is bringing their tried and true method of securing the best value electricity supply to non-residential customers of all sizes.

 

We can now provide residential electricity though our website Grand Prize Energy
